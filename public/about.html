Trust me, this is an important process. I've attempted to skip the crawling phase. It just doesn't work. I turned my attention to online courses and bootcamps. My ultimate goal is to take a coding bootcamp and get a certificate of completion. That's one giant leap towards reeling in the big bucks. Until then, I've started a course on Udemy, taught by Dr. Angela Yu. This course is on Web Development. I've realized web development clicks with me so much. I'm enjoying this journey, and I can't wait to be able to share all of my skills with potential clients and employers!
